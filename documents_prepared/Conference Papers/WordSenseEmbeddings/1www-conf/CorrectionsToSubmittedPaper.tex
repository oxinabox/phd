\paragraph{Page 4, Section 3.2}

Equation 4 in the submitted paper reads:
\begin{equation*} \label{eq:generalwsd}
P(s_{i} \mid \c) =
\dfrac{P_S(\c \mid s_{i})P(s_{i})}
{\sum_{s_{j}\in\s} P_S(s_{j} \mid \c)P(s_{j})}
\end{equation*}

Equation 4 should read:
\begin{equation*} \label{eq:generalwsd}
P(s_{i} \mid \c) =
\dfrac{P(\c \mid s_{i})P(s_{i})}
{\sum_{s_{j}\in\s} P(\c \mid s_{j})P(s_{j})}
\end{equation*}

Change: Replacement of $P_S(s_j \mid \c)$ with $P(\c \mid s_j)$ in denominator of last line.
